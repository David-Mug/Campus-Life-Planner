About Campus Life Planner

Campus Life Planner helps students organize their academic and extracurricular activities efficiently.

Track your tasks, set time goals, and manage your campus life in one place.

Features

  • Task management with due dates and duration tracking
  • Tag-based organization system
  • Dashboard with statistics and trends
  • Daily target tracking
  • Advanced search with regex support
  • Data import/export capabilities
  • Fully accessible and responsive design
